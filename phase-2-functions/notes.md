## Clinical Connection
Drug interaction computation mirrors nuclear fusion —
combining elements and calculating what emerges.
Cyclophosphamide + Doxorubicin = MODERATE severity,
increased cardiotoxicity risk. Pharmacist must monitor
cardiac function and limit cumulative doxorubicin dose.
